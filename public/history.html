AO Globe Life - Our Story At A Glance

From AIL roots and union solidarity to Globe Life strength and AO leadership

1951

Founding and early mission of AIL

  • Founded in Indianapolis by Bernard Rapoport and Harold Goodman to protect working families.
  • Premium waivers for union members during strikes, scholarships, and strike relief funds.
  • Guiding belief: "Be Union - Buy Union" with union-label operations.

Check out AIL's Who We Serve.

1961

Union-first innovations

  • Waived premiums during authorized strikes; contributed to strike funds.
  • Launched scholarships for union families; deepened labor alignment.
  • Set foundation for official AFL-CIO Union Label recognition in the 1970s.
Learn more

Check out AIL History.

1950s - 1970s

Growth and values

  • By 1956 AIL operated in 13 states with 300+ agents; moved HQ to Waco in 1958.
  • Coverage portability supported workers who moved jobs.
  • Scholarships and strike waivers reinforced solidarity in action.

Check out AIL History.

1970s - 1990s

Labor Advisory Board and community engagement

  • Established AIL/NILICO Labor Advisory Board with 65+ union leaders and AFL-CIO reps.
  • Regular advisory on wage security, safety, and healthcare access with feedback loops.
  • Award-winning Labor Letter amplified labor news and solidarity actions.
  • Hands-on support: picket lines, food banks, scholarships. 100% union-label under OPEIU Local 277.

Check out AIL Community and explore more outreach highlights on our Community page.

1994

Acquisition by Torchmark

  • AIL acquired by Torchmark Corporation for approximately $563M - added financial strength and scale.
  • Bernard Rapoport remained active to preserve mission and culture.

Check out About AIL.

2011 - Present

Community outreach - Youth baseball and softball grants

  • Globe Life and the Texas Rangers Baseball Foundation fund youth baseball and softball across a five-state region.
  • More than $625,000 distributed to 78+ organizations - impacting 75,000+ kids in 57+ communities.
  • Funding for fields, scoreboards, equipment, uniforms, and league development.

2014 - Present

Brand recognition and partnerships

  • Naming rights to Globe Life Field - home of the Texas Rangers (extended through 2048).
  • Official Life Insurance of the Dallas Cowboys; partnerships with Atlanta Braves, FC Dallas, Allen Americans.

Who is Globe Life?

Globe Life Inc. (NYSE: GL) is among the largest life insurers in the U.S. with over 17M policies and ~$229B coverage in force. Family of companies includes AIL, Liberty National, Family Heritage, United American, and more.

  • Strong ratings and decades-long track record of financial strength
  • Brand partnerships: Globe Life Field (Texas Rangers), Dallas Cowboys, Atlanta Braves, FC Dallas, Allen Americans

Check out Globe Life - About.

Inside Globe Life Field

Globe Life Field in Arlington delivers a climate-controlled, retractable-roof home for the Texas Rangers with the flexibility to host baseball, concerts, and marquee events year-round.

  • 1.8M-square-foot ballpark with a 5.5-acre retractable roof and operable outfield doors for open-air game days.
  • Seats approximately 40,300 fans with 360-degree concourses, premium clubs, and Texas Live! entertainment next door.
  • Designed for player wellness with advanced training facilities, natural light, and modern clubhouse spaces.

Who we work with

AO Globe Life partners with member-centric groups and organizations to expand access and serve working families.

  • Labor & trade unions: International Association of Fire Fighters (IAFF), International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW), United Steelworkers (USW), Communications Workers of America (CWA).
  • Public service & first responders: American Federation of Government Employees (AFGE), Fraternal Order of Police (FOP), National Association of Letter Carriers (NALC).
  • Community & service organizations: Veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW), American Federation of Teachers (AFT), United Way community campaigns and similar member benefit groups.
