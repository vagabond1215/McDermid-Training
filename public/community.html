Community Outreach & Impact

Beyond our historic milestones, AO Globe Life invests time, talent, and resources into programs that strengthen working families and the communities we serve.

Texas Rangers MLB Youth Academy

Globe Life supports the 17-acre West Dallas academy where young athletes receive free instruction, college prep, and wellness resources in a safe, year-round environment.

  • Hosts baseball, softball, and STEM programming for students across North Texas.
  • Partners with community mentors to deliver tutoring, workforce skills, and nutrition workshops.
  • Serves as the home base for the Junior RBI League and special events like the Jennie Finch Classic.

Globe Life × Rangers Equipment Grants

Since 2011, Globe Life and the Texas Rangers Baseball Foundation have provided crucial funding so kids can keep playing the sports they love.

  • More than $625,000 awarded to over 78 youth organizations in five states.
  • Investments cover scoreboards, uniforms, safety gear, and league program costs.
  • Grant recipients collectively serve more than 75,000 young athletes each season.

Fields for the Future ballpark upgrades

Community ballfields across Texas receive turf, lighting, and clubhouse upgrades through the Globe Life and Rangers “Fields for the Future” initiative.

  • Annual projects revive neighborhood diamonds for Little League and high school teams.
  • Recent renovations include new bullpens, digital scoreboards, and ADA-accessible seating.
  • Local volunteers join AO Globe Life associates for clean-up and field maintenance days.

See the 2023 project lineup

National Medal of Honor Museum pledge

Globe Life committed $20 million to help build the National Medal of Honor Museum in Arlington, honoring service members and investing in character education.

  • Funding supports exhibit design, educational outreach, and scholarship endowments.
  • Medal of Honor recipients guide curriculum that reaches classrooms nationwide.
  • AO Globe Life volunteers participate in veteran-focused events alongside museum staff.
