Community Outreach & Impact

Beyond our historic milestones, AO Globe Life invests time, talent, and resources into programs that strengthen working families and the communities we serve.

Labor Advisory leadership in action

Through the AIL/NILICO Labor Advisory Board, union leaders guide where support is needed most and keep us accountable to working families.

  • Advisory board brings together 65+ leaders from the AFL-CIO and affiliated unions.
  • Focus on wage security, workplace safety, and healthcare access with continuous feedback loops.
  • Hands-on solidarity: picket line support, food bank activations, scholarships, and 100% union-label operations.

Globe Life × Texas Rangers youth grants

Since 2011, Globe Life and the Texas Rangers Baseball Foundation have teamed up to give young athletes the resources they need to play and grow.

  • More than $625,000 awarded to 78+ organizations across five states.
  • Funding supports fields, scoreboards, uniforms, equipment, and league programming.
  • Impact reaches 75,000+ kids in 57+ communities.

Organizations we proudly support

AO Globe Life aligns with mission-driven groups that advocate for members and communities.

  • Labor & trade unions: IAFF, IBEW, United Steelworkers, Communications Workers of America.
